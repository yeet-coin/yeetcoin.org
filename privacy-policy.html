Privacy Policy for YeetCoin.org

Last Updated: July 26, 2025

The privacy of our website visitors at yeetcoin.org is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy aims to clarify the practices related to the collection, use, and protection of information on our website ("the Website").

Purpose of the Website:

YeetCoin.org is purely an advertising and informational platform for the YeetCoin digital currency. Our goal is to provide information about YeetCoin and guide valuable visitors to official platforms and websites where they can purchase YeetCoin or obtain additional information. We do not sell, trade, or host any direct purchase or investment operations on our Website.

1. Information We Do Not Actively Collect:

We adhere to the principle of not collecting personal information. Therefore:

  • **We do not request personal information:** We do not ask our visitors to register, create an account, or provide any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable details.
  • **We do not collect sensitive financial information:** We do not collect or process cryptocurrency wallet addresses, private keys, transaction details, or any other sensitive financial data from our website visitors.
  • **We do not store chatbot conversation data:** Our automated chatbot operates entirely client-side (i.e., in your browser) and does not store or transmit any of your conversations or inquiries to our servers or any third party.

2. Information Collected Automatically (Non-Personal Data):

Like most websites, some non-personal information may be automatically collected by our hosting server due to the nature of internet protocols. This information does not identify you personally and is not used to track you individually.

  • **Server Logs:** Our hosting service may collect standard server logs that include information such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent on each page, and visit times. This data is used for website security, maintenance, and overall performance improvement.
  • **Aggregated Usage Data:** We may analyze aggregated and anonymized data about how the Website is used (e.g., total number of visitors per page). This data is statistical and does not identify any individual.
  • **Language Preferences:** Your language preferences may be temporarily saved in your browser to enhance your experience during the current visit, but we do not permanently store these preferences on our servers.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

YeetCoin.org does not set persistent cookies to track user behavior across websites or collect personal information. Any temporary session cookies or use of browser local storage are for purely technical purposes related to the Website's operation during your current visit and do not involve the collection of personal data.

We do not use third-party analytics tools (like Google Analytics) or advertising cookies that might track your online activity.

4. Third-Party Links and External Platforms:

Our Website provides links to external websites and platforms (such as PancakeSwap, BSCScan, MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Bitget Wallet, OKX Wallet, X / Twitter, cryptocurrency price services). These links are provided to guide you to the official resources for YeetCoin.

Important Disclaimer:

  • We do not control the privacy practices or content of these external websites.
  • Once you click on these links and leave YeetCoin.org, our Privacy Policy no longer applies.
  • We strongly advise you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.
  • We are not responsible for any data collection, practices, content, or actions performed by these external websites or services. Our role is limited solely to guidance and direction.

5. Data Security:

Although we do not collect personal information, we take reasonable measures to protect the security and integrity of our Website. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy:

Our Website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under this age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.
